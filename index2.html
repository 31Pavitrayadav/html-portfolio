Angela's Recipe for he Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

for the filling:

    2/3 cup dark brown sugar
    1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
    ¼ cup butter

Instructions

    Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
    Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
    Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
    Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
    the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
    After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
